## What is Data Visualisation?

> The graphical representation of information and data

- **Purpose**: Communicate information clearly and efficiently
- **Goal**: Enable understanding, analysis, and decision-making
- **Medium**: Charts, graphs, maps, dashboards, infographics

---

## Why Visualise Data?

| Raw Data | Visualisation |
|----------|---------------|
| Hard to spot patterns | Immediate pattern recognition |
| Cognitive overload | Reduced mental effort |
| Limited working memory | Extended external memory |
